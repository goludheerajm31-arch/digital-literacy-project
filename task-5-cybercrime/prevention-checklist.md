# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## Safety Tips for College Students

- Use strong and different passwords for all accounts.
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Do not click unknown links from emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
- Verify website URLs before entering login details.
- Avoid logging into important accounts using public Wi-Fi.
- Keep your phone and laptop updated with security updates.

## Financial / UPI Safety

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone.
- Do not accept unknown UPI collect/payment requests.
- Always check the receiver’s name before sending money.

## Report Cybercrime
